blueprints were tried in private and only the winner is on display. Section~6
answers with the public trial ledger of removed law families and frozen
programs, and the score pays a ten-thousand-fold search penalty anyway; the
posterior lands at one in a million. Second: perhaps some locks were opened by
copying the combination. Any output whose calculation touched measured data is
scored at zero evidence in this budget, however well it lands, the way a
weather forecast issued after looking out the window scores nothing. What
survives both deductions is the number in the box.}

The reading is direct. One finite premise, frozen once, emits the discrete
architecture of the Standard Model, the continuous spacetime of general
relativity, and a scoreboard of numerical endpoints that measurement keeps
confirming, while its recorded rejections show the machinery is willing to
lose. Under any generous accounting of trials, coincidence is the losing
explanation by six orders of magnitude. The remaining alternatives are the
stated open gates, and each carries a registered test. That is the sense in
which we most likely inhabit an OPH simulation: the hypothesis is cheap, the
evidence is overconstrained, and every escape route is a named, checkable
computation.
